Sports Injury Management

Upchar Physiotherapy treats problems which are related to the muscles, bones, ligaments or tendons. Our experienced therapists are highly skilled in diagnosing the issue and also excellent in dealing with the treatment and rehabilitation right after the Orthopaedic/ sports injuries and surgeries. We truly care about your safe recovery. The techniques that we use here are of top-quality. Our techniques and approaches involved in Orthopaedics and Sports Injuries to make the recovery faster are strength training, joint mobilizations, taping, strengthening and and electrical stimulations etc. We truly care about your safe recovery that's why these programs are tailored according to the individual needs.

Our mission is to help every human being by promoting health and well- being and make them feel comfortable, relaxed and lively in their bodies.
