About our clinic

Our Mission

Our GOAL is to build a strong foundation for long term health goals. Our Therapists create a comprehensive treatment program that will help our clients to accomplish their therapeutic goals. We not only focus on treating their current condition but also eliminate the unseen future health issues by evaluating, assessing, diagnosing and treating. Our team of skilled therapist use advanced techniques to enhance fast recovery to patients.

Why choose us ?

Senior and Experience Doctor

Our Clinic offers a skilled team providing top quality health care and patient education. Years of experience give us the expertise needed to deal with complex health challenges. We promise to listen to your concerns, provide you with personalized treatments and encourage you to be an active participant in your health.

Advanced Technology

Our Rehabilitation Clinic in Brampton treats a wide variety of conditions including sports injuries, motor vehicle accident injuries, WSIB, back pain and much more. Here at Upchar Physiotherapy - Brampton our team of experts includes Chiropractors, Registered Physiotherapists, Registered Massage Therapists, Physiotherapy Assistants and Exercise Therapist/ Trainers.

First Time Free Consultation

When you visit UpcharPhysiotherapy in Brampton, for your initial assessment, you should expect to be at our facility for approximately 45 to 60 minutes. We ask that you arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment; this allows you to have ample time to fill out all the initial paperwork necessary.

Fully Licensed and Highly Qualified

Our healthcare team is highly qualified, with years of experience and depth of knowledge, all geared towards providing the most effective and comfortable treatment for you. A complete and comprehensive assessment first determines whether chiropractic therapy or physiotherapy is right for you.
